Safe & Secure

Why Blood Donation Matters

Blood Components

One donation can be separated into red cells, plasma, and platelets, helping multiple patients with different needs.

Quick Process

The entire donation process takes only 10-15 minutes. Your body replaces the donated blood within 24-48 hours.

Health Benefits

Regular blood donation helps reduce the risk of heart disease and improves overall cardiovascular health.

Medical Screening

Every donation includes a free health check-up, including blood pressure, pulse, and hemoglobin level testing.
