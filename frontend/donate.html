How to Register as a Blood Donor

Follow these simple steps to complete your blood donor registration

1

Fill Personal Details

Enter your full name, age, gender, and contact information accurately. Make sure all details are correct as they will be used for verification.

2

Select Blood Group

Choose your correct blood group from the dropdown. If you're unsure, please get a blood test done before registration.

3

Provide Location

Enter your city and state to help us connect you with nearby blood banks and patients in need of blood.

4

Choose Donation Date

Select your preferred date for blood donation. You can always reschedule later if needed.

5

Submit Registration

Review all information and submit your registration. You'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

6

Get Verified

Our team will verify your details and contact you to schedule your first donation appointment.
